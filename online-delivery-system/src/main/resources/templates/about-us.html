How to use the delivery service

  1. Sign in if you have an account
  2. Click on the order button in the banner
  3. Select the desired menu item for the selected day
  4. Once the order has been placed you will be given an email reciept.
  5. As easy as that, you food is ordered and enjoy your healthy meal!
